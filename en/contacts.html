Contact Moldiroda – Ceramic Factory in Vila Verde, Braga, near Barcelos

We are available to answer your questions, provide quotes, schedule visits or handle custom orders. Moldiroda is a handcrafted ceramics factory located in Vila Verde, near Barcelos in the Braga district, specializing in the production of clay tableware, terracotta pots, and handmade decorative pieces.

We work with professional clients from around the world, with a strong focus on resellers, interior design stores, restaurants, design projects, and brands looking for authentic, high-quality ceramics.

Location

Our factory is located in Cervães, Vila Verde, just minutes from Braga and Barcelos – regions with a strong ceramic tradition. We welcome visits from both national and international clients looking for reliable suppliers of locally made handcrafted ceramic pieces.

Ceramic Supply and Resale

If you're looking for a Portuguese ceramics factory for continuous supply, custom-made pieces, or resale, we are ready to respond with flexibility, quality, and professionalism. We work with both catalogue products and made-to-order pieces.
