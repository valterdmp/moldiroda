Wholesale Ceramic Products - Factory in Portugal

We are Portuguese manufacturers of handcrafted ceramics, based in Vila Verde, near Barcelos, in the district of Braga. We supply a wide range of ceramic pieces for wholesale, all handcrafted by experienced ceramicists. With over 20 years of experience in the sector, we work with resellers, home decor shops, hotels, architects, and distributors worldwide.

Our pieces are made using terracotta, ensuring durability, artisanal identity, and great commercial value.

Range of ceramic products:

We produce a variety of items including:

  • Terracotta pots for indoor and outdoor use, available in various sizes
  • Clay tableware
  • Decorative pieces in handcrafted ceramics
  • Customized items with logos, engravings, or exclusive finishes
