Handcrafted Ceramic Factory in Vila Verde, Braga, near Barcelos

Welcome to Moldiroda, a handcrafted ceramics factory located in Vila Verde, in the Braga district of northern Portugal, near Barcelos, a region with a strong tradition in Portuguese pottery.

We specialize in producing unique terracotta pieces, handmade with dedication, precision, and tradition. Our ceramics are 100% Portuguese, offering quality, durability, and a distinct identity.

Portuguese Ceramics for Resale and Interior Design

We produce a wide range of ceramic items, ideal for resellers, interior design shops, brands, designers, and hospitality projects. If you're looking for handcrafted ceramics for resale, we have the experience and capacity to meet your needs.

Our pieces combine tradition and authenticity. We use carefully selected raw materials and refined processes to ensure a high-quality finish.

Over 20 Years of Ceramic-Making Expertise

With more than two decades of experience, Moldiroda blends traditional Portuguese pottery techniques with modern production methods. Each piece is handmade by skilled ceramicists, ensuring consistency, authenticity, and quality in every order.

Our ceramics are crafted with care and passion, from the potter’s wheel to the artisanal shaping and final finishes.

Supplier of Handcrafted Ceramics

At Moldiroda, we work with professional clients from all over the world. We offer flexible solutions for wholesale, direct supply, and custom pieces.

We are a trusted partner for companies that value Portuguese ceramics and seek a reliable manufacturer committed to quality.
