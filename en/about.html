About Us – Ceramic Makers and Traditional Pottery Factory in Portugal

We are a Portuguese company dedicated to the handcrafted production of ceramic pieces, with over 20 years of experience in ceramic manufacturing in Portugal.

Our factory is located in Vila Verde, near Barcelos, in the Braga district – a region renowned for its deep connection to traditional pottery. We combine manual techniques with modern processes to ensure each piece is unique, authentic, and of high quality.

Our Story and Heritage

The ceramic tradition of Barcelos is a key part of the region’s cultural identity, and at Moldiroda we take pride in continuing this legacy. From the beginning, we have been committed to preserving traditional Portuguese pottery methods, valuing craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Our Products

At our ceramic factory in Portugal, we produce a wide variety of pieces, ranging from functional to decorative items:

  • Handcrafted terracotta and clay ceramics
  • Traditional and contemporary tableware
  • Decorative vases and jars
  • Artistic pieces and objects ideal for interior design
  • Products for resale in shops, brands, commercial spaces, or décor projects

Each piece is carefully crafted — whether on the potter’s wheel, by hand-molding, or through semi-industrial techniques — ensuring durability and an artisanal character.
